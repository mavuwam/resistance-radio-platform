# Product Overview

Zimbabwe Voice is a dual-purpose platform combining:

1. **Advocacy Platform**: Enables Zimbabweans to create and sign petitions defending democracy, constitutional term limits, and good governance
2. **Resistance Radio Station**: Community radio platform with shows, episodes, articles, events, and live streaming

## Core Mission

Empower citizens to organize, mobilize, and make their voices heard on critical issues affecting Zimbabwe's democracy, with a focus on protecting constitutional term limits and democratic freedoms.

## Key Features

- Petition creation and signature collection with real-time tracking
- Radio shows with on-demand episodes and live streaming
- News articles and community resources
- Event management and registration
- Content submission system for community contributions
- Admin dashboard for content management
- Secure authentication with role-based access control

## Target Users

- Zimbabwean citizens (domestic and diaspora)
- Content managers and radio hosts
- Community organizers and activists
- Platform administrators
