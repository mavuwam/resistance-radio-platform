## ‚ôø Theme 5: Accessible & Responsive

**Visual Style**: Clear, inclusive, high-contrast

**Colors**:
- Background: Pure white (#ffffff)
- Primary: High contrast orange (#ff6600)
- Focus: Blue outline (#0066ff)
- Text: Pure black (#000000)

**Key Features**:
- WCAG AAA compliant high contrast
- Large, touch-friendly buttons (44px minimum)
- Clear focus indicators (3px outline)
- Underlined links always
- Mobile-first responsive design
- Reduced motion support
- 18px base font size
- System fonts for maximum compatibility

**Best For**: Maximum reach, diverse audiences, mobile users, accessibility

**Vibe**: Like a government website - clear, accessible, functional

**Why Consider This**: Ensures maximum reach across all devices and abilities. High contrast works well in various lighting conditions (important for African markets). Mobile-first is crucial for areas with primarily mobile internet access.
